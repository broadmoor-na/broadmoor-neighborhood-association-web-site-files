Home Tour

Annual San Leandro North Area Home and Garden Tour: The tour feature beautiful homes in the San Leandro North Area.

The annual Home & Garden Tour is a wonderful way to spend a Sunday afternoon, get decorating ideas, meet old and new neighbors, and help our community. Proceeds benefit local schools and neighborhood beautification projects.

BNA History Room Tour

The Broadmoor Neighborhood Association pemyStyledically sponsors an historical night at the San Leandro Library for those who are interested in researching the history of their homes.

The San Leandro History Room owns a collection of rare books and materials on local history and the Broadmoor Neighborhood.

BNA members will have the opportunity to learn about their homes during the presentation given by the history room librarian.

This is an excellent opportunity join fellow Broadmoor neighbors for a fun fact filled evening and see if your home once belonged to a famous person.
