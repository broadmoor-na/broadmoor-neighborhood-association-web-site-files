San Leandro North Area Home And Garden Tour

Sunday, June 10, 2012, 12:00 pm

Zocalo Coffeehouse, 645 Bancroft Ave, San Leandro, CA | Get Directions »
$25

The 20th Annual San Leandro North Area Home and Garden Tour is set for June 10 from 12 to 4 p.m.

In addition to the tour of beautiful homes and gardens there will be a gift basket raffle and silent auction at this charity event.

Tickets can be purchased from Zocalo, Sabino's and Evergreen Nursery for $25 each.

This is a self-guided 2 to 3 hour tour. You can learn more about it by calling Patty Silva at 510-636-4137 or e-mailing slhometour@aol.com.
