Neighborhood trash pick-ups


Alameda County Industries no longer does a neighborhood trash pick-up. However, Alameda County Industries (the garbage collectors) allows its customers to individually schedule a pick-up of unwanted items once a year. It is collected on your normal garbage pick-up day and must be requested two weeks ahead of time. Just call Alameda County Industries at 357-7282 to schedule your pickup.
